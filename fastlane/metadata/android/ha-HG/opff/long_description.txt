A bincika kayayyakin abincin dabbobi ko lambar lambar su don samun bayanai akan sinadaran, abubuwan ƙarawa da abubuwan abinci masu gina jiki.

Wannan app tabada damar duba fiye da 600 Pet abinci kayayyakin riga kunshe ne a cikin free da kuma bude database Open Pet Bayanan abinci, da kuma don ƙara hotuna da bayanai don bata kayayyakin.

Don gano kaddamar da taimako dan buɗe bangare na Abinci, ziyarci https://world.openpetfoodfacts.org

*** Ka ba da gudummawa wajen tabbatar da kwaskwarima ***

- Bude Abubuwan Abinci shine tsarin samar da abinci wanda kowa yake yi, don kowa.
- Kuna iya amfani da shi don yin zaɓin abinci mafi kyau, kuma kamar yadda yake buɗe bayanan, kowa na iya sake amfani dashi don kowane dalili.
- Batun Bayanan Abinci wani shiri ne na riba wanda dubunnan masu ba da agaji suka kirkira daga sassan duniya. We have lots of exciting projects you can contribute to in many different ways. 
- Kuna iya fara bada gudummawa a yau ta ƙara samfuri daga kabad naku. 


*** Get the Facts ***

Hakanan zaka iya samun (bisa ga alamun da aka gabatar akan samfuran):
- jerin kayan masarufi (gami da abubuwan ash)
- bayanin abinci mai gina jiki, hasken zirga-zirgar abinci mai kyau…
- brands, allergens, tasirin (Organic, vegan...)
- Yana aiki tare da abincin kare (busheshen abincin kare & rigar kare abincin), abincin kulya, abincin zomo, abincin maciji, abincin kifi, abincin tsuntsu, abincin doki ko kowane abinci don dabbar da kuka fi so.

*** Works well in any condition ***
- You can add new products, even if you don't have an Internet connection right now.
- The app loads only the strict necessary to reduce data and battery consumption
- You don't need a camera to look up a product. You can also type the barcode if you lack a camera or have an issue with it.

*** Abincin abincinku, bayananku ***
- Bayananka naka ne, kuma ba a taɓa aika shi ta yanar gizo ba
- You can use the app anonymously
- You can export your scan history


Don abincin ɗan adam, zaku iya shigar da Faɗin Abinci Mai Kyau, don kayan kwalliya, Buɗe kayayyakin kyau, da sauran samfurori duka, bude samfuran Kayayyaki.
